About Me

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio, my name is Fabian. I started out my penetration testing journey back in the Fall of 2021. With no hacking experience, I sought to become a professional penetration tester, all within a year timeframe. After working through various offensive security labs, and achieving various certifications, I landed a role shortly after obtaining the OSCP. I am now an offensive security professional with 4+ years of experience spanning network, application, AI/ML, and cloud penetration testing.

I conduct adversarial assessments against generative and predictive AI systems — including prompt injection, indirect prompt injection through agentic pipelines and RAG architectures, and model inference attacks — alongside traditional application and infrastructure engagements.

Outside of work, I train regularly at the gym and compete in combat sports — I hold a blue belt in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. When I'm not on the mats, I unwind with anime and video games. My most recent obsession is the Resident Evil series.
